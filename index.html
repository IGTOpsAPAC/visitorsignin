💬 Teams notifications

Add a Teams webhook URL per employee so they get a Teams message when their visitor arrives. You can add these manually below or include a TeamsWebhook column in your employee CSV.

Add / update webhook
How to get a webhook URL in Teams:
  1. Open Microsoft Teams
  2. Click ⋯ next to the channel you want
  3. Select Workflows
  4. Choose "Post to a channel when a webhook request is received"
  5. Sign in, pick your channel, click Add workflow
  6. Copy the URL generated and paste it above
Can't find Workflows on the channel? In Teams left sidebar click View more apps, search for Workflows, open it, go to Create tab and search "When a Teams webhook request is received".
